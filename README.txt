Data Visualization Coursework 2020/04

The project is a research of solving the traffic congestion of New York City.

This is a website establishing by HTML, CSS, Javascript and D3.

Dataset: Traffic Volume Counts in New York City (2014 - 2018) -- since it is not complete enough, i only selected data in 2017

Methodology: 
  1. Compare the total traffic volume with different road types by bar plot
  2. Compare the total traffic volume about different roads by bar plot
  3. Compare the traffic volume for each road in a day by line chart
